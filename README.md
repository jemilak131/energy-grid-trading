# Energy Grid Trading Platform

A decentralized peer-to-peer energy trading platform that connects renewable energy producers with consumers, enabling direct energy transactions while maintaining grid stability and automated settlement.

## Overview

This platform revolutionizes the energy market by enabling direct peer-to-peer trading between renewable energy producers and consumers. Built on blockchain technology, it eliminates traditional intermediaries, reduces costs, and incentivizes renewable energy production while ensuring grid stability and fair pricing.

### Peer-to-Peer Energy Trading
- **Direct Producer-Consumer Matching**: Connect renewable energy producers directly with consumers
- **Dynamic Pricing**: Market-driven pricing based on supply, demand, and grid conditions
- **Real-Time Trading**: Instant energy trade execution with automated settlement
- **Energy Certificates**: Verified renewable energy certificates for green energy trading
